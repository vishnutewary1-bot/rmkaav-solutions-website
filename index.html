(01) — Who we are

We ship in weeks, not quarters.

What we are. RMKAAV is a one-person studio backed by three deep collaborators. We take on projects the big agencies can’t move fast enough on, and projects the freelancers can’t hold the full shape of.

What we do. Brand systems that survive their first A/B test. AI automations that replace six Zapier tabs with one dashboard. Editorial sites that don’t look like templates — because they aren’t.

Who we’re wrong for. If you need 40 meetings and a Confluence wiki to approve a hex code, we’re not your studio. If you want to see working code by week two and live traffic by week six, we probably are.

Case №03
Fintech AI SaaS

SnapLedgerX

RoleProduct · AI pipeline · Web app Year2025 Live atsnapledgerx.com

Challenge. Traders keep trade journals in spreadsheets and forget to log trades. Without pattern analysis they repeat the same losing moves.

Approach. An AI-powered trading journal that auto-logs via broker APIs, analyses performance with a GPT-4 pipeline, surfaces behavioural patterns (revenge trading, overexposure, holding losers), and serves a daily coaching note.

Next.js Postgres GPT-4 Broker APIs TradingView
Builder note The analytics layer isn't a dashboard — it's a coach. Every morning the journal writes one paragraph: "Yesterday you held SOL for 4 hours past your normal window. This is the 3rd time this month." That's the product.

NGO · Credit ledger · Rewards
Case №04
NGO Loyalty engine Awareness

Har Ghar Vijaya

RolePlatform · Reward engine · Cross-integration Year2025 Live athargharvijaya.org

Challenge. An awareness-action NGO wanted real-world contributions (task completion, advocacy, outreach) to translate into tangible benefits — with a natural tie-in to THC Store's wellness products as redeemable rewards.

Approach. A credit-based participation engine: users complete verified actions, earn credits, redeem them as discounts on partner products. Unified auth + credit ledger across the NGO site and THC Store.

Firebase Auth Firestore ledger Shopify discount API Cloud Functions
Builder note Credits are a dangerous primitive — easy to game, hard to reconcile. Built an append-only ledger with signed server-side transactions so no client can fake earnings. Redemption is atomic: credit burn + discount-code creation in the same Cloud Function.

— FAQ

Common questions,
direct answers.

Do you do retainers or only projects?

Both. SMM is a monthly retainer. AI automation and web design are project-based with optional ongoing support blocks once you’re live.

What happens if we disagree mid-project?

Weekly demos mean disagreements surface in week one, not week six. Bundle revisions are included; a major pivot triggers a fresh SOW so nobody is surprised by the invoice.

How many revisions are included?

Unlimited during the build phase, 30 days of revisions post-launch, then either a monthly support block or hourly rate after that.

Do you work with startups pre-revenue?

Yes, on reduced scope. Either an equity conversation, or ~40% off the rate card for friends-of-friends intros. We don’t discount blind.

What’s your tech stack?

Vanilla / React / Next.js · Three.js · GSAP · Firebase / Supabase · Shopify · GPT-4 · Make / Zapier · Solidity + wagmi when Web3 fits. No forced stack — we pick what fits the problem.

Do you handle hosting & ongoing maintenance?

Hosting yes (Vercel / Netlify for sites, Firebase / Supabase for apps). Maintenance via a monthly retainer or a per-hour block.

Can we see work-in-progress?

Always. Every project gets a staging URL from day 3. Weekly Loom walkthroughs during build. You see the ugly first drafts, not a polished reveal at the end.

Who owns the code?

You do. Full transfer of repo access, deploy secrets, and third-party accounts on final payment.

How long do projects take?

SMM is ongoing. AI automation is typically 4–6 weeks. Web design is 5–8 weeks depending on scope. Anything bigger (multi-product, marketplaces, dApps) quoted individually.

Where are you based?

Mumbai. Most work is remote; happy to meet in person for kickoffs or workshops if you’re local.
